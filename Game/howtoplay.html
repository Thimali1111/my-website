How to Play

1. The game consists of 5 levels, each with an image-based question.

2. Each level has a 1-minute time limit.

3. The goal is to correctly identify and enter the solution for each question before time runs out.

4. Click a number button to submit your answer.

5. If your answer is correct, you'll gain 20 marks. If your answer is incorrect, 10 marks will be deducted.

6. If time runs out before you submit an answer, you have to start from level 1.

7. Your current score is displayed at the top of the game screen.

8. To return to the main menu, click the "Arrow" button at the top of the game screen.
